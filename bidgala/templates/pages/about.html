"Power in Community"

Bidgala is building a community of artists, design professionals and art lovers to help bridge the gap between business and art. Through our online platform, we provide a transparent marketplace that facilitates meaningful connections between each member of the art community. From emerging to established, Bidgala aims to empower artists by providing them with the support they need to own their careers. At Bidgala, we champion artists by putting their stories at the heart of our community.

"Innovation through Education"

Bidgala is a creative ecosystem that expands the art community through education and conversation. Our mission is to keep art-lovers informed on current happenings in the art-world, all while driving new and innovative ideas. Read our weekly editorials and newsletters for interviews, stories, tips, tricks, and much more.

"Democratizing Art"

By creating an accessible platform, we want to make the artworld more accessible to art lovers and artists everywhere. Our barrer-free marketplace welcomes people from all walks of life and seeks to inspire the current and future generations of artists and art enthusiasts. At Bidgala, we believe in the power of community and that together, we can spark change one connection , one sale and one opportunity at a time.

Our Story


William Lande & Sam Tenenbaum co-founded Bidgala out of Montreal, Canada when they noticed a pair of art students struggling to sell their work. The struggle for artists was intensified when the COVID-19 pandemic caused business closures and in-person event cancellations. William & Sam made it their mission to support all artists and to share their stories.
