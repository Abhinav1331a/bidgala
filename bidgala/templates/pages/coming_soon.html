About Us

Bidgala is an online art marketplace:
Bidgala is an online art platform where art buyers can purchase art directly from artists without paying gallery prices. Bidgala’s simple web navigation helps users buy & sell within minutes.

Bidgala is a powerful art Community:
Network with artists, art buyers and professionals by using Bidgala’s chat, follow, like, and comment features.

Bidgala Supports Artists- low commission, artist exposure, and art education:
Bidgala is artist friendly and has low commissions of 30% which are used to promote your art. Also take advantage of Bidgala’s artist support services which offer artists exposure and expertly produced art education content.

Bidgala supports art buyers:
Bidgala’s low art prices, variety of art, and simple web navigation help to make designing your home affordable, personalized, simple, and easy. Buy sculptures, buy photographs, buy paintings and more on Bidgala. Our live chats and expert curators are also there to help you make your home design easier.

Bidgala supports Professionals:
Are you someone who buys art for clients? Benefit from the Bidgala partners program and get 10% commission on every piece you buy. Also take advantage of our private page for interior designers and home stagers where we’ll list your business information for all our users to see.

Want to find local artists?
Use Bidgala’s “Art Stores Near Me” feature to find local artists and discover art you’ll love! Find Canadian Artists, American Artists, UK artists, and more. Better yet, refine your search by province or state! Also check out Bidgala’s sections for Student art, African American art, Queer art and Womens art.

Bidgala Referral Program:
Signup, refer your friends and get 5% cash on everything they buy and sell for an entire year!
