What is the 100% money-back guarantee on art for sale?

How is artwork shipped?

All orders are packaged and shipped by the individual artists you order from.

An artist must accept the order within a max of 5 business days from receiving your order. The artist then has 5 business days from accepting your order to ship the artwork and submit a tracking number. If either of these deadlines are not met by the artist, your order will automatically be canceled and you will never be charged.

You can find your order’s tracking number in the orders page or in your chat with the artist. Click on the tracking number for your order’s ETA.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Because artworks are shipped directly from artist to buyer, the length of the delivery period will vary. Tracking numbers will be submitted to you by the artists to the buyers once the item is shipped so that you can check your order’s ETA at any time.

You can find your order’s tracking number in the orders page or in your chat with the artist. Click on the tracking number for your order’s ETA.

When will I be charged for the artwork I purchase or bid on?

You will not be charged until the artist sends you a tracking number. The artist has a maximum of 5 business days to send you a tracking number after accepting the order.

Example: if the artist accepts your order but does not send you a tracking number, you will not be charged.

Are artworks already framed?

Depending on the listing, the art you order may or may not be framed. Make sure to read the descriptions of artworks carefully before ordering and message the artist with any questions you have beforehand. You can also message the artist to confirm.

What countries can artworks be shipped to?

Because artworks are shipped directly from independent artists to art-lovers, whether or not something can be shipped to a certain region depends on each artist and their listing.

When you find a piece you’re coveting, make sure to check which countries they ship to. You can also search for artwork based on regions the artists ship to.

What if the artist doesn’t ship to my region?

Since each artist can select which countries they ship to, they may or may not ship to your region. Your best bet is to send them a message explaining where you are located and that you want to buy their work. Ask if they can make an exception for you and create a new listing for the piece.

What about taxes and duties?

You may be required to pay taxes and duties to claim the work that you order, depending on where the artist ships from and where you are located. Since Bidgala is a global community, we have no way of calculating taxes and duties for every location. Therefore, please contact your customs bureau for specific information. Normally, extra taxes and duties only apply to artworks you purchase from outside your country.

How & when do I get paid when I sell my art?

To initiate your payment when you sell your art, follow these 4 steps:

  • Connect Stripe to your account via your Settings Page.
  • Accept the order in your Orders Page.
  • Ship your art & collect a tracking number
  • Submit the tracking number for your shipment in your Orders Page.

You will be paid by buyers via Stripe, therefore, you cannot accept orders until you have connected a Stripe account to your Bidgala settings (step 1). You can sign up to stripe via your settings page.

Once you accept the order (step 2), ship your art (step 3), and then submit the tracking number (step 4), your funds will be released into your Stripe account.

Stripe is similar to Paypal, and depending on your location and payout schedule, funds from your sales will be paid out to your bank account, from your Stripe account, on a rolling basis. So you may be able to see “future” payouts in your Stripe balance, but the money will not be in your bank yet. This is because it hasn't reached the payout date, which Bidgala estimates at two to three weeks.

Email us at info@bidgala.com for any questions about Stripe.

What are your fees or commissions?

There are zero membership fees at Bidgala and it is free to post your art. Here is how our commission works:

  • 30% transaction fee on the total amount of your listing (Price + Shipping). Fees may differ for international transactions and foreign exchange transactions.
  • Stripe fees for processing your payout safely are estimated to be 2.9% + $0.30. Fees may differ for international transactions and foreign exchange transactions.
  • Formula: Your final income = (Art price + Shipping fee) x (0.671) - $0.30.

Keep in mind that you choose your prices and can adjust them upwards to get what you want in the end. For example, if the price of your art is $1550 and you charge $100 for shipping, you will end up with $1106.85 in your Stripe account.

Why do you take commission on shipping?

We know that it may sound bizarre to take commission on shipping. However, we think you’ll understand once you hear our reasoning. Bidgala’s commission used to be 35% with zero commission on shipping. However, many users were charging their entire art prices in the shipping section and putting $0 in the price column to avoid commissions. Therefore, we lowered the commission to 30% on the total transaction to account for this issue.
